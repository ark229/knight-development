How the Process Works

PLAN| DESIGN | BUILD | LAUNCH

1. Consultation

During this stage we will focus on your personal and business journey, website strategy, content strategy, prototyping, design, and much more to help achieve a vision around your specifications.

2. Proposal

After the consultation, you will be presented with a proposal that will explain the solution, give an overview of the project and an estimated timeline of completion, outline the process, and explain the next steps. You will also be presented with detailed contract information and pricing.

3. Development & Testing

Once the goals have been defined, the design is planned, the branding elements are created, and the content plan has been devised – it is time to BUILD YOUR WEBSITE! During this stage, your website will also be tested thoroughly to check the functionality of all elements. The goal is to detect any issues like compatibility problems with different devices or broken links to ensure everything works properly.

4. Launch & Support

Testing is complete and now your website is ready to be deployed onto a live server and officially launch! Our relationship will not end there, because as your business grows, you may need to increase the number of pages, add more functionality, or update features. Knight Development is here to offer you continued maintenance and support.
