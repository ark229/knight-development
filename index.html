About

Your trusted tech partner—right in the neighborhood!

I’m passionate about helping entrepreneurs, small businesses, and mission-driven teams grow through smart, sustainable web and tech solutions. How the Process Works

PLAN| DESIGN | BUILD | LAUNCH

1. Free Consultation

We’ll begin with a personalized conversation to explore your goals, whether that’s building a website, launching a digital product, improving your data systems, or developing a smart tech strategy. I’ll learn more about your business journey, identify key challenges, and start shaping a vision that aligns with your mission.

2. Custom Proposal

After the consultation, I’ll send you a tailored proposal that clearly outlines your solution. This includes a project summary, timeline, pricing, deliverables, and a process overview. Whether you’re hiring me for development, training, or strategic guidance, you’ll have full clarity before we begin.

3. Development & Execution

Once we agree on the scope, I’ll get to work, whether it’s building your website or app, developing documentation, delivering a workshop, or implementing your tech roadmap. You’ll receive regular updates, and I’ll involve you in key decisions to ensure alignment. Everything is thoroughly tested or reviewed to ensure high quality and effectiveness.

4. Launch & Long-Term Support

Once the project is complete, I’ll help you go live or transition smoothly into implementation. But the partnership doesn’t end there. As your business grows, I’m available for future updates, feature additions, consulting sessions, or training support. Knight Development is here to grow with you!
